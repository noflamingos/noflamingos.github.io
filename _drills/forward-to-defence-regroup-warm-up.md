short_description: "Warm-up focusing on timing and passing for forwards and defence."

images:
  - "/assets/images/drills/forward-to-defence-regroup-warm-up/1.png"
setup: |
  Split F into both corners with pucks and D in the neutral zone split into LD and RD. Drill is run in pairs alternating sides.
full_description: |
  F1 skates up ice as D1 skates into the zone across the blue line and F1 passes to D1. F1 continues to skate up the ice around the neutral zone face-off dot and then curls back towards the net to receive a pass. When D1 receives the pass, they pivot backwards and skate with the puck, then pivot forwards and pass back to F1. The drill repeats with F2 passing to D2. Players return to the line they started in.
coaching_points:
- Forwards need to time their loop so they can receive the pass back as they cross the blue line.
- Defence must complete their regroup quick enough to be able to make a pass back to the forward.
