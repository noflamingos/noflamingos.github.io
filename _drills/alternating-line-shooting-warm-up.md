title: "Alternating Line Shooting Warm-up"
short_description: "Warm-up drill for forwards with give-and-go passes."
thumbnail: "/assets/images/drills/alternating-line-shooting-warm-up/thumb.png"
images:
  - "/assets/images/drills/alternating-line-shooting-warm-up/1.png"
setup: |
  Line-up forwards in two groups just inside the blue-line (avoiding the defence in the NZ).
full_description: |
  F1 skates into the slot, takes a shot on net, and skates towards the opposite line. F2 makes a pass to F1 who passes right back as F2 skates into the slot for a shot on net. The drill repeats going line to line.
coaching_points:
- Players must take shots before the hashmarks and quickly transition to be available for a pass from the opposite line.
- Players should adjust their speed so they receive the pass before getting to close to the net.
- Players should monitor for pucks in each line and a single player can go to collect pucks as required throughout the warm-up.
