short_description: "Point shot followed by a full-ice 2v1."

  - "/assets/images/drills/end-to-end-2v1/1.png"
setup: |
  Split the F into opposite corners and the D on opposite blue lines. Pucks into the corners with the F. Pylons down the middle of the ice and one at the face-off dot opposite of the F. The drill is run out of both corner simultaneously.
full_description: |
  On the whistle F1/F3 passes to the D who skates to the middle and takes a shot on net. F1/F3 get to the net for a tip and then skates around the far pylon to swing low back across the ice. F2/F4 skates towards the face-off dot while F1/F3 swings wide in front of them and then pass them the puck along the boards. Both F complete a two-on-one up the ice.
coaching_points:
- If the puck crosses over the centre line, the drill on that side of the ice is dead (players should not cross the pylons to get a puck). A coach may pass a puck to the F to continue the drill.
- F in the corner should skate out of the corner towards the middle of the ice before making a pass towards the boards.
- F1/F3 must swing all the way across the ice and will receive the pass back as they skate up the boards.
- The D should keep one F wide while preventing a pass.
- Once there is a shot on net or the D clears the puck away, the drill is done.
