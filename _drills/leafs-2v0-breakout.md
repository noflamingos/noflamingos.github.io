title: "Leafs 2v0 Breakout"
short_description: "Flow drill with a breakout, 2v0, and point shot."
thumbnail: "/assets/images/drills/leafs-2v0-breakout/thumb.png"
images:
  - "/assets/images/drills/leafs-2v0-breakout/1.png"
setup: |
  Line up four groups of F in the neutral zone facing towards the goal and two groups of D inside each zone facing towards the goal. Pucks in opposite corners and pylons through the neutral zone to split the ice in half. This drill is run on both sides of the ice.
full_description: |
  On the whistle, pairs of F curl back into their zone with one F pivoting back towards the boards and the other curling low (like a C). The D picks up a puck, skates around the net and passes to the F on the boards. As the F move through the neutral zone they should follow their pass (weave) until reaching the blue line when one forward stays high in the slot for a pass and the other drives wide and passes them the puck for a shot on net. The D follows the play to get up to the far blue line. The F who makes the pass curls into the corner, picks up another puck and passes to the D before driving to the net. The D moves the puck into the middle and takes a shot with both players battling with each other to put in the rebound. 
coaching_points:
- F must curl low enough to be able to receive and make a breakout pass before exiting the zone.
- F should make at least one pass through the neutral zone before attacking wide.
- This drill is intended to be run at full speed. If F are going slow, consider adding a back checker.
