setup: |
  Presuming the Ws are left- and right- shot, have them flip sides (if not, align normally). The C is trying to win the draw back towards the boards to the W.
full_description: |
  The net-front W should identify and line-up against the opponent W to tie-up and delay them getting to our D. The board-side W should be prepared to skate across the top of the circle (and adjust as needed) to pick up the puck with options to pass to the D or drive to the net for a shot.
coaching_points:
- The net-front W should identify and line-up against the opponent W to tie-up and delay them getting to our D.
- If the opponent stacks both W in the middle of the ice, our net-front W should tie-up up the W cutting across the ice.
- Both D should be prepared to move the puck towards the middle of the ice (either skating with it laterally or making a pass to their partner, if available).
- After the face-off, Ws must remember to flip back to their original side.
