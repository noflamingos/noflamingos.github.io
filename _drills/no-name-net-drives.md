title: "No Name Net Drives"
short_description: "Two net drives and two point shots with a screen."
thumbnail: "/assets/images/drills/no-name-net-drives/thumb.png"
images:
  - "/assets/images/drills/no-name-net-drives/1.png"
setup: |
  Line-up forwards in the corner and defence at the blue line. Pucks in the corner and a net (or obstacle) near the blue line. A coach can be in the face-off circle to apply pressure.
full_description: |
  On the whistle the F makes a pass to the D and quickly receives the pass back. The F will drive across the top of the circle and take a shot on net. As they curl around the circle, the next F in line spots them a puck and they will continue up the wall before punch turning and escaping low to drive to the net for a second shot. After taking the shot they will stay in front of the net for a screen/tip.

  The second F in line will make a pass to the D who will take a quick shot on net. The F makes a second pass and the D will walk the line around the net/obstacle for a second shot on net. 
coaching_points:
- F should work on continuing to move their feet as they drive to the net and take their shot.
- F should shield the puck from the coach.
- When walking the line, the D should practice a fake shot or pass as if they are facing pressure from an attacking F.
