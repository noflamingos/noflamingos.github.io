## Purpose
- Retrieval habits and shoulder checks
- Quick first pass with feet moving
- F support timing and spacing

## Setup
- One D starts below the goal line.
- One F supports low, one F on the wall.
- Coach spots a puck behind the net.

## Execution
1. D retrieves and scans.
2. First pass to low support.
3. Low support moves it to wall or middle option.
4. Exit with speed.

## Progressions
- Add a light forechecker.
- Require 1-touch on the wall.
- If breakout fails, regroup and try again.

## Coaching cues
- “Scan early”
- “Low first”
- “Feet through the pass”
